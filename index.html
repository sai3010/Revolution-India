About Us

Project JATAYU

This project is about building an IT solution to help women in distress and adopt a holistic approach in resolving issues that women face in all walks of life. Like the mythical bird from Ramayana, who valiantly fought with Ravana and sacrificed himself to rescue Sita, we Jatayus at RevolutionINDIA want to make a significant difference by protecting our women, children and anybody in distress, ourselves.

What is JATAYU?

Anybody who cares enough to volunteer for the safety of their fellow beings is a JATAYU.
Every Jatayu is selected by extensive background check along with police verification.
In a country like ours with a huge area and population, we cannot expect police to be available everywhere, so let's take it in our hands to help our people in distress.

How will we do that?

Technology! Your smartphone will be the key.
When in distress, at the touch of the screen on our app, an SOS call will be made and every Jatayu in less than 5 minutes radius will be alerted and directed towards the person in distress with the help of live tracking on the device.

What can you do to help us?

    Become a JATAYU by supporting us and downloading our app which is yet to be launched

            and spread the word.
